County of Monroe Industrial Development Agency

The County of Monroe Industrial Development Agency (COMIDA) performs a number of tasks for Monroe County, NY. A full description of what the organization does can be found on their website:

http://www.growmonroe.org/

While conducting business throughout the year, COMIDA has public meetings. These meetings have agendas, minutes, and resolutions that are posted publicly in PDF format to their website. These various meeting notes date back to 2007.

This website allows for the simple searching of all of the available meeting agendas, minutes, and resolutions available on the COMIDA website. Note: due to the nature of how the PDFs were created by COMIDA, some preview text may look garbled and incorrect. The links on this site link directly to the COMIDA website, where the original PDFs can be found in a a human readable format.
